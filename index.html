Selena Lee is a dancer and choreographer with a deep interest in somatics, which have been the focus of her studies along with the connection between dance and meditation. with 7+ years of mindfulness meditation experience, she leads meditation and movement sessions at dance studios and community spaces.
sola system is a Nigerian Brooklyn-based DJ with a penchant for black diasporic sound. Raised between three continents, borders and genres aren’t boundaries when they weave sonic tapestries  - from global club and bad bitch rap to percussive bass and shimmery house, their goal is to make you move.

they are driven by a desire to connect QTPOC communities through music. sola system is a co-founder of ABEG NYC, a collective by and for queer Africans, and SLICK DOWN, a collective focused on electronic music’s roots in black culture. they are a resident on 8 Ball Radio and host their party HYPNOSIS quarterly.
